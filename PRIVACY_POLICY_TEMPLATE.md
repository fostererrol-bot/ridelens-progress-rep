### Introduction

Welcome to RideLens ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the RideLens mobile application and related services.

RideLens is designed to help you track and analyze your ride data by uploading screenshots. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

---

### Information We Collect

#### 1. Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- User profile information (if provided)

#### 2. Ride Data
- Screenshots you upload
- Extracted ride metrics (distance, time, power, etc.)
- Timestamps of uploads
- Data you manually enter

#### 3. Usage Information
- App usage statistics (anonymized)
- Device information (device type, OS version)
- Crash reports (if app crashes)

#### 4. Information We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT automatically pull data from third-party services
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts
- We do NOT collect data about other apps on your device
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels for advertising

---

### How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve RideLens services:

1. **App Functionality**
   - Process and store your uploaded screenshots
   - Extract and display ride metrics
   - Generate trends and comparisons
   - Sync data across your devices

2. **Account Management**
   - Create and maintain your account
   - Authenticate your login
   - Provide customer support

3. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Improve data extraction accuracy

### How We Share Your Information

**We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.**

We only share data in these limited circumstances:

1. **Service Providers:** Supabase (database hosting) - subject to their privacy policy
2. **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
3. **With Your Consent:** If you explicitly choose to share data (e.g., export feature)

### Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

#### Access & Export
- View all your data within the app
- Export your data (Settings → Export Data)

#### Correction
- Edit or correct your information through the app
- Update your profile anytime

#### Deletion
- Delete individual ride entries
- Delete your entire account (Settings → Delete Account)
- Upon account deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days

#### Portability
- Download your data in standard format
- Transfer to another service if desired

### Children's Privacy

RideLens is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
